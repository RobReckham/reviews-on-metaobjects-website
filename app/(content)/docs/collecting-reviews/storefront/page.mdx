# Storefront review form

Storefront reviews are submitted directly by your own review forms and app blocks.

## Behavior

- New submissions appear in your app review data.
- Submission quality and moderation follow your app settings.
- Reviews can be published immediately or reviewed first, depending on your preferences.

## Verification and context

- Request-based flows can carry extra context to improve trust signals.
- Direct storefront submissions still work without external source sync.

## Publish status

Use <a href={`${process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_APP_ADMIN_URL}/settings`} target="_blank" rel="noopener">app settings</a> to decide whether submissions are auto-published or moderated before display.

## Troubleshooting

- If submissions are accepted but not visible, check moderation/publish settings first.
- If forms submit inconsistently, ensure the app block is mounted once per page.
- If trust context is missing, verify request-based flows are using valid request links.
