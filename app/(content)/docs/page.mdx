# Introduction

FiveOh Reviews on Metaobjects helps you collect and display customer reviews in Shopify.

## Start here

1. Open the app in Shopify admin.
2. Enable and place your app blocks in the Theme Editor.
3. Connect at least one review source.
4. Confirm reviews are visible on storefront pages.
